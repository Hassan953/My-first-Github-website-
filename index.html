How To Put A Video On Your Website

Step 1

The first step is you have to send the video from your phone to your computer.

Step 2

To encode the video open "Adobe Media Encoder". From there you will need to change some things about your video like,

  • Change size to 720 x 480
  • speed to 30 frames per sec
  • Aspect - Square Pixels
  • Profile - Baseline
  • Level 3.0
  • Bitrate encoding: VBR 2 pass
  • Target Bitrate - 1.5
  • Max Bitrate 1.8
  • Use Max Render

    • you will also need to change your audio to:

  • AAC
  • Sample Rate 44100
  • Mono
  • Bitrate - 80

    How To Use Brackets

    Brackets are what tell your computer what to do. They are the most important things when making a website.
    Learning brackets is basically learning a new language, different brackets do different things. Some of the brackets i know how to use are, heading brackets, paragraph brackets, paragraph dividing brackets, style brackets etc.
    when you are writing a website you first put the name of the bracket you want to use, for example if i wanted a heading i put h1 inside of brackets and then /h1 inside brackets after it. the number you put is how big the text will be. if you want to divide a paragraph you put a br in brackets but for this one you dont need an end tag.
